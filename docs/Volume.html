Volume

If the Room survey mode is activated you'll get after each shot the following screen:

If you don't want to survey the volume at that particular station you can select SKIP and come back to the survey of the line.

The item after SKIP is the frequency at which measures are taken. You can choose between 1,2 and 5 measures per second or Manual Mode. In manual mode you will have to move the slider Upwards to take each measure. Otherwise the measurements will be automatic.

Aim at the place you want to start scanning the room and select TARGET. A RED rectangle will appear on the right. No measure is taken until the device is stable.

Once the measures are taken a WHITE rectangle will start blinking for each new measure. The total number of measures will appear in the bottom right corner.

Dots will also appear on the map, depending on the mode selected.

You can PAUSE and RESUME the process at will.

When paused the process will show a YELLOW rectangle At any point you can STOP the process which will save the data or RESET it which will start it over.

In case you get a BLUE rectangle displayed that means that the distance continuity constraint has been broken. Slow down the movement until it returns to blinking WHITE.

Consider not making more than a quarter of turn in 10s either laterally or vertically.

Each volume survey can contain a maximum of 2000 points.
