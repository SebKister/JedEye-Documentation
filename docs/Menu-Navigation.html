Menu Navigation

Each menu will have an active or selected item that will be surrounded by >….< or appear highlighted.

Moving the slider downwards (Next command) is shifting the selected item to the next one (Either on the right or beneath).

Moving the slider upwards is equivalent to the “Enter” key of your keyboard. It selects the current item or starts the action highlighted or selected.
