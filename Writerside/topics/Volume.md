# Volume


If the Room survey mode is activated you'll get after each shot the following screen:

![screencap_2025-12-29_15-50-11.png](screencap_2025-12-29_15-50-11.png)

At that point if you don't want to survey the volume at that particular station 
you can select **_SKIP_** and come back to the survey of the line.

The item after SKIP is the frequency at which measures are taken. You can choose between 1,2 and 5 measures per second or Manual Mode.
In manual mode you will have to move the slider **_Upwards_** to take each measure.
Otherwise the measurements will be automatic.

Aim at the place you want to start scanning the room and select **_TARGET_**. 
A _RED_ rectangle will appear on the right. No measure is taken until the device is stable. 

![screencap_2025-12-29_15-50-32_mod.png](screencap_2025-12-29_15-50-32_mod.png)

Once the measures are taken a _WHITE_ rectangle will start blinking at each measure taken.
The total number of measures will appear in the bottom right corner.

![screencap_2025-12-29_15-50-57.png](screencap_2025-12-29_15-50-57.png)

Dots will also appear on the map, depending on the mode selected.

You can **_PAUSE_** and **_RESUME_** the process at will.

![screencap_2025-12-29_15-51-11.png](screencap_2025-12-29_15-51-11.png)

When paused the process will show a _YELLOW_ rectangle
At any point you can STOP the process which will save the data or RESET it which will start it over.

In case you get a BLUE rectangle displayed that means that the distance continuity constraint has been broken.
Slow down the movement until it returns to blinking WHITE.

![screencap_2025-12-29_15-50-50.png](screencap_2025-12-29_15-50-50.png)

> Consider not making more than a quarter of turn in 10s either laterally or vertically.
