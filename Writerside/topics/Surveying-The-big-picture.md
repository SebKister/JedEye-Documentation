# Surveying - The Big Picture

> **Work in Progress**
>
> This section will provide a high-level overview of the surveying workflow using the JedEye.

The surveying process involves:
1.  **Calibration**: Ensuring sensors are accurate.
2.  **Data Acquisition**: Measuring distance, azimuth, and inclination.
3.  **Data Management**: Storing and retrieving survey shots.
4.  **Export**: Transferring data to a computer or mobile device.

Specific details for each step can be found in the subsequent sections.
