# Switching JedEye ON

There's no **ON/OFF** switch on the device. You'll switch the device ON using the slider control described in the
previous section.

- Move the slider control **upwards** and hold it there until a confirmation message appears. At
  that moment move the slider **downwards** and the device will turn on displaying a splash screen and the firmware
  version.
  
![screencap_2025-12-29_15-46-30.png](screencap_2025-12-29_15-46-30.png)

> Tip: You have around 4s to validate the ON command. If not done on time the device will
> turn off again. This procedure is useful to avoid unwanted switch on during transport.

- When this appears, it means JedEye has booted properly, and all the sensors are working.
  Move again the slider **upwards** and you’ll be in the main menu.

![screencap_2025-12-29_15-46-52.png](screencap_2025-12-29_15-46-52.png)

> Tip: You can deactivate the requirement to move the cursor to the right with the **Safe On**  Device Setting.
