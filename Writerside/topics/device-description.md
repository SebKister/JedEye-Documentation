# Device description

Thank you for your interest in the [JedEye](https://www.arianesline.com/mnemo/)!
We will start with a short description of the components of the device

![image_1.png](placeholder.png)

### 1 - Slider Control
It's through this slider that you're gonna interact with the JedEye.
- Moving the slider **Up** is the equivalent of an ***Enter*** or ***Select*** order.
- Moving the slider **Down** is the equivalent of a ***Next*** or ***Scroll*** order.
### 2 - Display
The device has an OLED graphical color display. The text, icon and background colors will guide you through the survey process.
### 3 - USB Port
This is the port used to connect your device to a 5V USB charger. It can also be used to connect the device to your computer to update the JedEye's firmware.
>The recommended way to download data is through Wi-Fi by activating it as Wi-Fi Access Point or by connecting your device to the local network.
### 4 - Laser Window
It's through this opening that the LIDAR emits laser light. (CLASS-II)
>Only clean the laser window port by using flowing water. **NEVER** use any chemicals or solvents. 
>**NEVER** try to rub it clean with a cloth.
