# Survey History and Direct View

You can access the history of your surveys on the device in the Menu in 
**OPTIONS > HISTORY**
It will display a list of all the surveys on your device. 

![screencap1702463019.png](screencap1702463019.png)

By selecting one of those surveys you'll get access to a 3D map of that survey.
The 3D map is interactive, it will rotate according to the orientation of the device.

![screencap_2025-12-29_15-53-27.png](screencap_2025-12-29_15-53-27.png)

Slider Upwards and you'll have a 2D view of the survey.

![screencap_2025-12-31_16-27-35.png](screencap_2025-12-31_16-27-35.png)

Once more slider upwards and you'll have basic statistics of the survey.

![screencap_2025-12-31_16-31-20.png](screencap_2025-12-31_16-31-20.png)

And one final click will bring you back in the History menu entry.


>These maps can also be displayed during survey when selecting the **_MAP_** menu entry.
