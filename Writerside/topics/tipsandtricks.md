# Tips and Tricks

- **Force Rebooting** : You can force the device to reboot by applying a small magnet on the back side of the device at the level of the display. This will turn the device off.

![](placeholder.png){width=320}
- **Battery Care** : To maximize battery lifespan, avoid letting the battery drain completely. If you are not using the device for an extended period, charge it at least once a month.
