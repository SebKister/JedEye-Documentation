# Calibration

The **Calibration** menu is crucial for ensuring accurate survey data.
Navigate to **OPTIONS > SETTINGS > CALIB**.

## Compass Calibration
Calibrates the magnetometer to compensate for hard and soft iron distortions.
- **Standard**: The recommended calibration routine. Follow the on-screen instructions to rotate the device in all axes.
- **Fast**: A quicker, less comprehensive calibration for field checks.
- **Stabilization Factor**: Adjusts the filtering applied to the compass readings. Higher values provide smoother but slower updates.

> **Tip**: Calibrate your compass whenever you change geographical locations significantly or modify the device's mounting configuration.

## IMU Calibration
Calibrates the Accelerometer.
- **Next Position**: Move through the required static positions (typically 6 faces of a cube) to calibrate the accelerometer.
- **Save Calibration**: Saves the calculated calibration constants to non-volatile memory.
